The official website of The Garden


Welcome to The Garden. The Garden is a community where plant hobbyist may learn, interact, and develop their skills on plant caring. The Garden aims to help plant owners take care of their plants properly via our community of plant keepers. You can share your experiences, ask for recommendations, and show the world your beloved plants. The Garden also aims to promote a cleaner and greener world, for our future generations to come.



How to join?

  • Click here to sign up.
  • Login here using your registered account.
  • Enjoy the experience and share your plant journey!
